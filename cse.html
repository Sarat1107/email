ABOUT EMAIL

omponents of an Email: Sender: The person who initiates and sends the email. Recipient: The person or group who receives the email. Subject Line: A brief summary of the email's content. Body: The main content or message of the email. Email Addresses: An email address typically consists of a username followed by the "@" symbol and the domain name (e.g., username@example.com). Types of Emails: Text Emails: Simple emails containing only text. HTML Emails: Formatted emails with multimedia elements. Attachments: Files (documents, images, etc.) can be attached to emails. Email Protocols: SMTP (Simple Mail Transfer Protocol): Used for sending emails. POP3 (Post Office Protocol 3): Retrieves emails from a server to a client.
